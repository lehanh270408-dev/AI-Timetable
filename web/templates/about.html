About SchedHub

SchedHub

is a student-built project created by Hai Anh, a high schooler passionate about applied mathematics and technology.

Designed to simplify the complex process of creating and managing timetables, SchedHub brings together automation, optimization, and usability. With a focus on both accuracy and accessibility, it offers tailored interfaces for schools, teachers, students, and parents — making communication and scheduling effortless.

Born from a desire to apply mathematical algorithms to real-world problems, SchedHub represents Hai Anh’s vision of how math and code can work hand in hand to improve education management.

MISSION AND VISION

SIMPLIFY

Automatically generate timetables, detect conflicts, and reduce manual operations to focus on education quality.

EMPOWER

Intuitive interfaces for each role; all information organized in a unified dashboard.

CONNECT

Connect diverse needs from high school to university with flexible modular design.
